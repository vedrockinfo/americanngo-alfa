Proposed Plan for the Pilot Project

purpose

To deepen students' math understanding, increase their interest & boost their overall progress.

Duration

30 day Pilot

Participants

Pre-K to Grade 2

Goals

To prepare children to learn independently

step 01

Baseline Assessment

  • Conduct an initial Baseline Survey to accurately gauge the starting skill levels and knowledge base of both participant groups.
  • Use your own country’s test materials OR use our customized tests + OMR sheet design
  • This foundational assessment will serve as a benchmark for effectively tracking and comparing progress over the course of the program.

step 02

Training

  • We can conduct online trainings for your teachers OR
  • An online training for your key resource people, who can then conduct an in-person training.
  • Our objective is to synchronize teaching approaches and maximize the efficacy of program delivery.

Teachers from Maldives and Nepal getting trained in ALfA pedagogy

step 03

Implementation

  • Phase 1 kicks off with a focused 30-day ALfA Numeracy implementation
  • This phase focuses on elementary mathematics, specifically the number system and basic operations: addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.
  • Concurrently, one group will implement the ALfA program while a control group continues with their regular studies.
  • At the end of the 30 days, the results from both groups will be compared to assess the effectiveness of the ALfA pedagogy.

step 04

Post Test

  • A post-test will be conducted for both groups after the implementation phase.
  • This assessment is designed to evaluate and compare the learning outcomes.
  • It measures the effectiveness of the ALfA approach against traditional educational methods.
  • Should the outcomes prove positive, the decision to advance to Phase 2 with the broader math concepts will be at the discretion of the government and other decision-makers.

How We Support Your Journey

Access to Online Resources

  • Baseline Survey
  • Mid-line Survey (provided upon request by the school)
  • End-line Assessment
  • Online Books

Training for Educators

  • Training sessions provided to help teachers understand and effectively implement our educational pedagogy.

Progress Monitoring

  • Head of School and teachers will have access to tools that enable them to monitor student progress both individually and across entire classes.

Impact and Future Prospects

  • Demonstrates measurable success in student performance and skill acquisition
  • Aims to expand globally, refining approaches
  • Prepares more students for tomorrow's world complexities
  • Continuous improvement to adapt to future educational challenges
